This is 9 - Rubidium’s Tuesday schedule! On this day, we wear our Type A or “Dengue” uniform, as instructed by the school.
Compared to Monday, which only has one study period, our Tuesday schedule gives us two — offering extra time
to catch up on tasks, review for upcoming quizzes, or simply take a break before gearing up for the next subjects to come.

For me, the Tuesday schedule is more manageable. It is more balanced, with a good mix of subjects
and study periods. Having one study period in the morning and one in the afternoon allows me to
manage my time better in executing my tasks, and helps me stay focused throughout the day.
